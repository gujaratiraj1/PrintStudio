Frequently Asked Questions About Printing Costs in Bangalore

Do you offer bulk discounts?
Yes! Bulk orders of 500+ get 10–20% discount. Monthly contracts get even better rates. Contact us for custom quotes.
Is delivery included in the price?
Free delivery within Bangalore for orders above ₹500. For smaller orders, ₹50–100 delivery charge applies. Walk-in pickup is always free.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, and cash. Payment after design approval and before printing starts.
How much does 3D printing cost in Bangalore?
Our FDM 3D printing rates generally range from ₹5 to ₹15 per gram, or ₹20 to ₹60 per machine hour, depending linearly on the material used (PLA, ABS, PETG, TPU) and the file's complexity. Small prototypes start at just ₹250.
Where can I find affordable visiting card printing near me?
If you are located in or around Bangalore, Sam's Print Studio provides highly affordable Visiting/Business card printing starting at just ₹149 for 100 matte cards, with same or next-day turnaround options available.
