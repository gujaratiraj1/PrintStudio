3D Printing FAQ

Common questions about our 3D printing services in Bangalore.

How much does 3D printing cost in Bangalore?
3D printing at Sam's Print Studio starts from ₹250 per hour of print time. Final cost depends on model size, complexity, infill percentage, and material. Small items like keychains cost ₹100–300, while architectural models can range from ₹1,500–₹5,000+. We provide free quotes — just send your STL file via WhatsApp.
What materials do you use for 3D printing?
We primarily use PLA (eco-friendly, strong, 20+ colors), PETG (heat-resistant, for functional parts), and ABS (for engineering prototypes). Special materials like TPU (flexible) are available on request.
How long does a 3D print take?
Small items (keychains, phone stands): 1–3 hours. Medium items (figurines, cases): 4–12 hours. Large items (architectural models): 12–48 hours. We typically deliver within 2–5 business days.
Can you 3D print from my own design file?
Yes! We accept STL, OBJ, and 3MF file formats. If you have a concept sketch but no 3D file, our team can help with basic 3D modeling for an additional fee.
What is a 3D lithophane and can you print one?
A 3D lithophane is a thin 3D-printed panel that reveals a photograph when backlit. It's a truly unique personalized gift. Yes, we specialize in lithophane printing — just send us a high-resolution photo. Starting from ₹500.
