Digital Services FAQ

How much does logo design cost in Bangalore?
Professional logo design starts from ₹999. Includes 2–3 concepts, 2 revision rounds, and final files in PNG, JPG, SVG, and PDF. Premium brand identity packages start from ₹2,999.
What is Google Business Profile and why do I need it?
Google Business Profile makes your business appear in Google Maps and "near me" searches. When someone searches "restaurant near me" or "printing near me," Google shows Business Profiles first. We set it up with photos, services, hours, and keywords so you get found.
