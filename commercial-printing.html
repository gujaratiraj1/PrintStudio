What We Print

Everything your business needs to look professional and attract customers.

Flyers & Pamphlets

A5 and A4 flyers on 130–170 GSM paper. Single or double-sided. Matte or glossy. Perfect for events, promotions, and door-to-door marketing. Same-day available.

Brochures

Bi-fold, tri-fold, Z-fold, and gate-fold brochures on premium art paper. Ideal for company profiles, product catalogs, and service overviews.

Posters (A3 & A4)

Vibrant full-color posters for events, promotions, and branding. Printed on sturdy art paper with optional lamination for outdoor use.

Booklets & Catalogs

Multi-page booklets with saddle-stitch or spiral binding. Training manuals, product catalogs, company profiles, and presentation documents.

Restaurant Menus

Durable laminated menus on 250–300 GSM card. Custom designs that match your restaurant's branding. Tearproof and spill-resistant options available.

Real Estate Brochures

Premium property brochures and presentation folders for builders and architects. Glossy finish with high-resolution photography printing.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cost of flyer printing in Bangalore?
A5 flyers start from ₹2–4 per piece for 100+ quantity. A4 flyers from ₹4–7 per piece. Pricing depends on paper quality, sides printed, and lamination. Bulk orders of 500+ get significant discounts.
What types of brochures can you print?
We print bi-fold, tri-fold, Z-fold, and gate-fold brochures in A4 and A5 sizes on 170–300 GSM art paper with matte or glossy lamination. Minimum order: 25 brochures.
Do you offer same-day printing for flyers?
Yes! Standard A5 and A4 flyers can be printed same-day for orders placed before 2 PM. Brochures and booklets typically require 1–3 days.
Can you print restaurant menus and real estate brochures?
Absolutely. We specialize in restaurant menu printing on 250–300 GSM card with lamination. Real estate brochures get premium art paper with glossy finish. Free design support available for both.
