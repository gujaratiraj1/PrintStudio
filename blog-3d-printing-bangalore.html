What is 3D Printing and Why Does Your Bangalore Business Need It?

3D printing (or additive manufacturing) is the process of creating three-dimensional solid objects from a digital file. Rather than cutting material away from a block (like traditional CNC machining), a 3D printer adds material layer by layer until the object is complete.

For local businesses and creators in India's tech capital, the benefits are game-changing:

  • Unmatched Speed: Go from CAD design to a physical, testable model overnight.
  • Cost-Effective Iterations: Catch design flaws early before spending lakhs on injection molding tooling.
  • Complex Geometries: Print intricate designs and internal cavities that are literally impossible to manufacture with traditional methods.

Our 3D Printing Technologies Explained

1. FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling)

  • How it works: Extrudes melted thermoplastic filaments layer by layer.
  • Best for: Rapid prototyping, low-cost proof-of-concept models, and functional mechanical parts.
  • Materials: PLA, ABS, PETG, and TPU (flexible).

2. SLA (Stereolithography)

  • How it works: Uses a UV laser to cure liquid resin into solid plastic.
  • Best for: High-detail miniatures, architectural models, and jewelry casting patterns.
  • Materials: Standard resin, tough resin, and clear resin.

Real-World Applications in Bangalore
