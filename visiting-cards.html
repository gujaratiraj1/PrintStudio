Visiting Card Options

Choose from a wide range of finishes, sizes, and paper stocks to match your brand identity.

Matte Finish Cards

Smooth, non-reflective finish. Professional and elegant. Perfect for corporate cards. Available in 300 GSM and 350 GSM art card paper.

Glossy Laminated Cards

High-shine finish that makes colors pop. Ideal for vibrant designs with photography or bold branding. Scratch-resistant lamination included.

Textured / Linen Cards

Tactile, premium feel with woven texture. Conveys sophistication and quality. Popular with lawyers, consultants, and luxury brands.

Eco-Friendly Kraft Cards

Brown kraft paper with a rustic, organic look. Great for eco-conscious brands, cafes, and artisanal businesses. 100% recyclable.

Spot UV & Foil Cards

Selective gloss highlights or metallic foil stamping on specific elements. Creates eye-catching contrast. Ultimate premium finish.

Custom Shapes & Sizes

Square, rounded corners, mini cards, or die-cut shapes. Stand out from standard rectangular cards with unique dimensions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about visiting card printing in Bangalore.

How much do visiting cards cost in Bangalore?
At Sam's Print Studio, visiting cards start from ₹149 for 100 cards (standard matte finish). Premium options with glossy lamination, spot UV, or textured paper range from ₹199–₹499 for 100 cards depending on the finish and paper quality. Bulk orders of 500+ cards get additional discounts.
Can I get same-day visiting card printing in Bangalore?
Yes! We offer same-day visiting card printing for orders placed before 2 PM. Standard visiting cards on matte or glossy finish are available for same-day pickup or delivery within Bangalore.
What paper options are available for business cards?
We offer multiple paper options: 300 GSM matte art card, 350 GSM glossy art card, textured linen paper, kraft/eco-friendly card stock, and premium metallic finish. All cards can be single-sided or double-sided with optional lamination.
Do you provide design support for visiting cards?
Yes, we provide free basic design support with every printing order. This includes layout design, logo placement, font selection, and print-ready file preparation. Send us your details via WhatsApp and our designer will create a proof for your approval.
What is the minimum order quantity for business cards?
Our minimum order is just 50 cards, making us ideal for freelancers, startups, and small businesses. Bulk discounts are available for orders of 500+ cards.
